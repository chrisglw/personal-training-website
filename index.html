About Me

Hi, I'm Christian

I'm an online and in-person fitness trainer. My fitness journey began in my teens with a few YouTube videos, and since then, I've been fascinated by the process of transformation, both physical and mental. I believe everyone has the potential to become their best self, and my mission is to inspire others to achieve that change.

Starting without a coach, I relied on self-education and faced my share of setbacks and injuries, which only fueled my passion to dive deeper into fitness. Over the last eight years, I've studied workout techniques, nutrition, and earned coursework in fitness and nutrition during college.

Let me help you skip the trial and error and avoid injuries by sharing what I've learned, so you can start your own fitness journey with confidence.
